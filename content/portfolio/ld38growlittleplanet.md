This game was my entry for compo section of Ludum Dare 38 under the theme "*Small World*".
<!--more-->
The goal is to explore space, destroy asteroids and collect materials to bring back to your planet to make it evolve.

The compo category requires to make a game alone in 48 hours and producing all assets by yourself during the period of of the jam.
