This game was made during the \"Pot-au-Jeu\" Game Jam, a french Game jam, a 48h competition. The theme of the jam was "*Makeing new stuff with old stuff*".
<!--more-->
The goal of the game here is to take dead limbs (the old stuff) to make brand new Dr. Frankenstein's style zombies.


The compo category requires to make a game alone in 48 hours and producing all assets by yourself during the period of of the jam.
