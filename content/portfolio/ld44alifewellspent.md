This game was made during Ludum Dare Game Jam in the __Jam__ category. The game was made in 48h with a team of 2 people.
The theme of the Jam was "*Your life is the currency*"
<!--more-->

The compo category requires to make a game alone in 48 hours and producing all assets by yourself during the period of of the jam.
