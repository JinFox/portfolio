This game was made during the Ludum Dare Game Jam #39 under the theme "*Running out of Power*".
<!--more-->
The goal of the game is to make sure none of the 'coffee thieves' will steal your coffee and finally find the last remaining cup of coffee in the office.
The last cup of coffee was made for the compo category of the Ludum Dare.

The compo category requires to make a game alone in 48 hours and producing all assets by yourself during the period of of the jam.
